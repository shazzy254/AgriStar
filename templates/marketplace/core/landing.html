About AgriStar

Bridging the gap between Kenyan farmers and the market.

AgriStar is dedicated to transforming the agricultural landscape in Kenya. We provide a comprehensive ecosystem that empowers farmers with AI-driven insights, connects them directly with buyers to maximize profits, and ensures consumers receive the freshest produce. From secure M-Pesa payments to trusted delivery, we are building a community of growth and trust.
